The subgraph in \( G'_1 \) formed by adding \((u_i, v_j)\) is isomorphic to the subgraph formed in \( G'_2 \) by adding \((w_k, x_\ell)\). Therefore, in the complements \( G_1 \) and \( G_2 \), removing those edges creates isomorphic graphs.
