\begin{tikzpicture}[every node/.style={draw, minimum size=0.8cm}]
    \matrix (m) [matrix of nodes, column sep=2.5cm, row sep=0.4cm] {
        1 & z_{-i_1} + \cdots + z_{-i_k} \\
        1 & z_{-i_2} + \cdots + z_{-i_k} \\
        \vdots & \vdots \\
        1 & z_{-i_k} \\
    };
    
    \matrix (n) [matrix of nodes, anchor=west, column sep=2.5cm, row sep=0.4cm, right=of m-1-2] {
        k & \\
        k-1 & \\
        z_{-i_k} & \\
        z_{-i_{k-1}} & \\
        \vdots & \vdots \\
        1 & \\
    };

    \draw[->] (m-1-2)--(n-1-1);
\end{tikzpicture}

The `partition of partial sums' and its conjugate associated to the partition $\sum\limits_{j=1}^kj\cdot z_{-i_j}$ where $i_k>\cdots>i_1$.
