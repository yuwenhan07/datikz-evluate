\begin{tikzpicture}[scale=2]
    % Define points
    \coordinate (p1) at (1.5,0);
    \coordinate (p2) at (2.5,1);
    \coordinate (p3) at (-1,0);
    
    % Draw the ellipse
    \draw[thick] (0,0) ellipse (1.5 and 0.8);
    
    % Draw the tangent line at p2
    \draw[dashed] (p2) -- ++(45:2);
    \node at (3.5,1) {$T_{p_2}\partial\Omega$};
    
    % Draw the triangle
    \draw[thick] (p1) -- (p2) -- (p3) -- cycle;
    
    % Label the points
    \fill (p1) circle (1pt) node[below right] {$p_1$};
    \fill (p2) circle (1pt) node[above left] {$p_2$};
    \fill (p3) circle (1pt) node[left] {$p_3$};
    
    % Label the domain
    \node at (0.5,0.5) {$\Omega$};
\end{tikzpicture}

A symplectic billiard bounce $(p_1,p_3)\mapsto (p_2,p_3)$ in a strictly convex domain $\Omega$.
