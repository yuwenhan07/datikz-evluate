Creating a colorful prism with a gradient coloring, a different base color, and letter labeling in TikZ can be quite an interesting task! Below is a step-by-step guide to achieve this. We'll use `tikz` along with `shadings` for the gradient effect.

Here's a complete example:

```latex
\documentclass[tikz,border=10pt]{standalone}
\usepackage{tikz}

\definecolor{baseColor}{RGB}{255, 165, 0} % Orange
\definecolor{topColor}{RGB}{255, 238, 0} % Gold

\begin{document}

    \coordinate (A) at (0,0);
    \coordinate (B) at (4,0);
    \coordinate (C) at (4,2);
    \coordinate (D) at (0,2);

    \coordinate (E) at (0,-1);
    \coordinate (F) at (4,-1);
    \coordinate (G) at (4,1);
